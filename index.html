Our Emote Marketing Tool processes inputs you provide (e.g., text, design preferences) to generate emotes. These inputs are processed client-side and are not stored by us after your session ends. We may collect:

We use collected data to:

We do not store or share your emote inputs. Any analytics data is anonymized and used solely to improve our services.

3. Third-Party Services

Our site and tool may use third-party services, including:

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law or to provide our services (e.g., analytics or ad serving).

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Disable cookies in your browser to limit analytics tracking (note: this may affect site functionality).
  • Contact us with privacy concerns (see below).

6. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@oceanscurse.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
